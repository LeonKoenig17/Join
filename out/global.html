getContactsFromFirebase()

Fetches contact and login data from Firebase, merges them, sorts the users, groups them alphabetically, and renders the grouped contact list in the navigation UI. Side Effects: - Modifies global variables: `userNumber`, `groupedUsers`, `dataLogin`, `dataContact`, `dataFull`. - Updates the DOM element with id "allContacts" by clearing its content and appending grouped user elements. Dependencies: - Assumes existence of `fireBaseContent` object with `login` and `contact` properties. - Relies on helper functions: `sortUsers()`, `createGroupDiv(letter)`, and `createSingleUserNav(user, userNumber)`.
